Who We Are..!!!

Established in Vadodara, NEEDS | New Era Engineering and Development and Solutions is a pioneer in manufacturing high-performance industrial machinery. We specialize in the design and fabrication of Vibro Sifter (Gyro Screen | Vibrator Screen | Chalna | Sifter) and Ribbon Blender, catering to the Pharmaceutical, Food Processing, Spices Industries, Plastic Industries, Minerals Industries, Ceramic industries and Chemical Industries.
