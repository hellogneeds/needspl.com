Who We Are..!!!

Established in Vadodara, (NEEDS) New Era Engineering and Development and Solutions is a pioneer in manufacturing high-performance industrial machinery. We specialize in the design and fabrication of Vibro Sifters and Ribbon Blenders, catering to the Pharmaceutical, Food Processing, Spices Industries, Plastic Industries, Minerals Industries, Ceramic industries and Chemical Industries.

"We understand your genuine NEEDS better."

Our engineering philosophy is rooted in precision and efficiency. We don't just build machines; we develop integrated systems that optimize production lines. By blending traditional craftsmanship with modern 3D-modeled precision, we ensure every piece of equipment is delivered in OPTIMAL operational status.
